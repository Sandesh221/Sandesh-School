About Us

    Sarah Academy is an independent,coeducational boarding and day school offering a comprehensive college-preparatory program to students in grade 9-12. Our school was founded in 1992 with state-of-the-art academic and athletic facilities. A community drawn from wide range from a wide range of traditions and backgrounds in the U.K and around the world. Sarah Academy extends opportunities for teachers and students to deepen their skills, find new passions and discover their best selves, With a faculty committed to creating a supportive and inclusive environment for intellectual, social, and personal development. We continues to fulfill our mission through enrolling a diverse, academically talented and well rounded student body. We Welcome students, faculty, and staff of all faiths.
